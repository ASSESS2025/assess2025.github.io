Welcome to ASSESS2025

This is the ASSESS2025 project, which focuses on advancements in the spatial assessment of socio-ecological systems. Our goal is to integrate innovative methodologies to understand and improve the connections between social and ecological factors in urban and rural environments.

Key Objectives

  • To develop new spatial assessment tools.
  • To analyze socio-ecological systems using advanced geospatial techniques.
  • To foster collaboration between researchers and practitioners in socio-ecology.

Feel free to explore the site and learn more about our ongoing work!
