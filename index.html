Welcome to the ASSESS Workshop

Join us in Istanbul at the ICCSA 2025 International Conference on Computational Science and its Application for an interactive workshop on the latest innovations in spatial assessment of socio-ecological systems.

About the Workshop

This workshop focuses on advancing spatial modeling and assessment of socio-ecological systems, with a particular emphasis on the role of green and blue infrastructures, and ecosystem and landscape services within urban environments. The aim is to bring together researchers to present and discuss cutting-edge techniques, methodologies, and digital tools that enhance our understanding of cities as interconnected SES.
